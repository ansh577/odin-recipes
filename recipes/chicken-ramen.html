Homemade Chicken Ramen

Home

Image by jcomp on Freepik

Description

A homemade recipe for chicken ramen to beat those soupy mid-day cravings! You will need a lot of vegetables and japanese cooking items for this, so be sure to visit your local asian grocery store. The umami-flavoured rich broth will be worth it! This recipe makes 4 servings.

The original recipe is by Dino Kodippili at theflavourbender.

Ingredients

Ramen (Shoyu):

  • 4-5 cups good quality chicken stock
  • 4 spring onions cut to 3 inch pieces
  • 5 cloves garlic sliced
  • 2 inch piece of ginger sliced
  • 4 fresh red chili (optional)
  • 1/4 cup light soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup mirin
  • 1 cup fresh shiitake mushrooms with stems trimmed and sliced
  • 6 pieces dried shiitake mushrooms (optinal)
  • 4 portions dried ramen noodles

Marinaded chicken:

  • 2 Tbsp mirin
  • 1 Tbsp dark soy sauce (or light soy sauce adjusted with salt)
  • 1 Tbsp light soy sauce
  • 1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 2 cloves garlic chopped finely
  • 4 pieces boneless chicken thighs

Glaze for chicken:

  • 2 tsp brown sugar
  • 2 Tbsp dark soy sauce (or 1 Tbsp light soy sauce + 1 Tbsp water)

Steamed greens:

  • 1 large bunch of yu choy or spinach
  • Soy sauce
  • Drizzle of sesame oil
  • 2-3 cloves of garlic finely sliced

Ramen toppings:

  • 4 spring onions finely sliced
  • Sliced radishes
  • 4 Ramen eggs or soft boiled eggs
  • Chili garlic oil

Steps

Making the Caramelized Soy Chicken:

  1. Place the chicken and the marinade ingredients in a bowl. Coat the chicken, and let it marinade for an hour.
  2. Preheat the oven to 425°C or 218°C. Place an oven rack on the top third, and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  3. Smooth side down, and evenly spaced, place the chicken on the baking sheet.
  4. Place the sheet in the oven, and cook for 15 minutes. In the meanwhile, prepare the glaze by mixing the brown sugar and soy sauce, and start preparing the ramen (the next section).
  5. After 15 minutes, flip the chicken, and spoon the glaze over, and let it cook for 10-15 more minutes. Once done, remove from the oven and let it cool down, then slice.
  6. Your chicken is done!

Making the Ramen:

  1. Prepare two saucepans.
  2. Place one saucepan on the stove (SP1). Add the stock, ginger, garlic, spring onions, chili (optional), soy sauce, and mirin. Stir, then cover the pot.
  3. Let SP1 come to a boil at medium-high/high heat. Lower to medium and let it simmer for 20 to 25 minutes. Make sure to taste test if it needs salt.
  4. If you want more flavour, add the dried shiitake mushrooms.
  5. Prepare the second saucepan (SP2). Add water and bring to a boil, then add the dried noodles. Cook according to the packet instructions (preferably a minute less than the instructions). Drain, and divide into 4 bowls.
  6. Once SP1's broth is done, strain to remove the spring onions and chili. If you added dried mushrooms, remove these, then slice them and set aside.
  7. Add the strained broth back into SP1, then add the fresh mushrooms, then let it cook for a further 5 minutes, till the mushrooms soften.
  8. Your ramen base is done!

Making the Eggs:

  • If you want ramen eggs, use this recipe.
  • Else, if you want boiled eggs:
    1. Boil water in a pot, then add 4 large eggs.
    2. Cook the eggs for 6 to 6.5 minutes.
    3. Immediately place the eggs in cool running water/an ice bath for a few minutes. Peel and set aside till you're ready to serve.

Making the Steamed Greens:

  1. Wash the greens, then cut in half to remove the thick stems.
  2. Place the greens in a bowl, then drizzle soy sauce and sesame oil. Add garlic, then toss to combine.
  3. You now have three options:
    1. You can cover the greens then microwave in 1 minute increments till the greens soften. Make sure they're not too wilted.
    2. You can heat a non-stick pan up with a little oil, then pan fry the greens with soy sauce and garlic for 2 to 4 minutes.
    3. You can blanche the greens in the ramen broth for a minute. After that, remove the greens, drizzle soy sauce, sesame oil, and garlic, then toss to mix.

Serving:

  1. Divide the hot stock between the four bowls. Do the same with the noodles.
  2. Top with the steamed greens, sliced soy chicken, eggs, spring onions, radishes, and other toppings that you'd like.
  3. You've finished your ramen!
