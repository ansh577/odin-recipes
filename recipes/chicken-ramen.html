Description

A homemade recipe for chicken ramen to beat those soupy mid-day cravings! You will need a lot of vegetables and japanese cooking items for this, so be sure to visit your local asian grocery store. The umami-flavoured rich broth will be worth it! This recipe makes 4 servings.

The original recipe is by Dino Kodippili at theflavourbender

Ingredients

Ramen (Shoyu):

  • 4-5 cups good quality chicken stock
  • 4 spring onions cut to 3 inch pieces
  • 5 cloves garlic
  • 2 inch piece of ginger sliced
  • 4 fresh red chili (optional)
  • 1/4 cup light soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup mirin
  • 1 cup fresh shiitake mushrooms with stems trimmed and sliced
  • 6 pieces dried shiitake mushrooms (optinal)
  • 4 portions dried ramen noodles

Marinaded chicken:

  • 2 Tbsp mirin
  • 1 Tbsp dark soy sauce (or light soy sauce adjusted with salt)
  • 1 Tbsp light soy sauce
  • 1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 2 cloves garlic chopped finely
  • 4 pieces boneless chicken thighs

Glaze for chicken:

  • 2 tsp brown sugar
  • 2 Tbsp dark soy sauce (or 1 Tbsp light soy sauce + 1 Tbsp water)

Steamed greens:

  • 1 large bunch of yu choy or spinach
  • Soy sauce
  • Drizzle of sesame oil
  • 2-3 cloves of garlic finely sliced

Ramen toppings:

  • 4 spring onions finely sliced
  • Sliced radishes
  • 4 Ramen eggs or soft boiled eggs
  • Chili garlic oil
